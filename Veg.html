Veg Soup

Vegetable soup is a common soup prepared using vegetables and leaf vegetables as primary ingredients. It dates to ancient history, and is a mass-produced food product in contemporary times.



Paneer Thikka

Paneer Tikka is a beloved vegetarian street food that’s popular across India. It consists of chunks of paneer, which is Indian cottage cheese, marinated in spices and yogurt, then grilled to perfection. It’s often served with a side of mint chutney and is a favorite for its smoky flavor and succulent texture.



Veg Briyani

Veg Biryani is a delightful Indian rice dish that combines fragrant basmati rice with an assortment of colorful vegetables and aromatic spices.
